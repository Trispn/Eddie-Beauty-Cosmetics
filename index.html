About Eddie Beauty Cosmetics

Welcome to Eddie Beauty Cosmetics, your go-to destination for all your beauty needs in Kampala, Uganda. At Eddie Beauty Cosmetics, we pride ourselves on offering a wide range of high-quality makeup, skincare, and beauty products that cater to every individual's unique style and preference.

Our Location

You can find us conveniently located at the Mini Price Building on Kikuubo Road in the heart of Kampala, Uganda. Our central location ensures easy access for all beauty enthusiasts seeking top-notch products and exceptional service.

Our Mission

At Eddie Beauty Cosmetics, our mission is simple: to empower our customers to embrace their beauty with confidence. We believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best, and we are committed to providing the tools and products necessary to achieve that goal.

Our Products

Explore our extensive selection of makeup, skincare, hair care, and beauty accessories sourced from renowned brands both locally and internationally. From luxurious foundations and vibrant lipsticks to nourishing moisturizers and rejuvenating serums, we have everything you need to elevate your beauty routine to the next level.
